OurVision
AI Club MITS Gwalior

Our team focus on enlightening every student at MITS who wants to explore AI through this club by sharing and learning together to build a place for like-minded people.

To share and learn about every aspect that the professionals in the industrial world have to offer to us will be among our preferences and to make them members versatile in all fields.

We want MITS to be a library for the students who want to achieve or foresee their future in the field of AI.

Club Activities


We believe that for a club to function properly, to increase our reach and student's understanding
more further, we have planned debates, discussions, seminars & webinars, tech workshops,
tech fests, drone workshops etc.

We want to build a platform that is open to all students for every AI enthusiast and promotes group
activities, increases work efficiency, clears out their brains for brighter ideas and increases their ability
to work with an extra edge.
